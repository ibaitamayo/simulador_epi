# Disclaimer

## Purpose

EPIDEM is an educational, research, and planning-oriented epidemiological simulation platform.

It is designed to explore population-level transmission dynamics, aggregated mobility, intervention scenarios, demographic structures, and health-system impact through computational simulation.

## Not a clinical decision-support tool

EPIDEM is not a medical device.

It is not intended for diagnosis, treatment, monitoring, prognosis, or direct clinical decision-making.

Outputs must not be used as the sole basis for decisions affecting individual patients, clinical care, emergency response, or operational public health action.

## Model limitations

Simulation results depend on model assumptions, user-defined parameters, input data quality, representativeness of data sources, and simplifications required for computational modelling.

Outputs should be interpreted as exploratory scenario results, not as forecasts or predictions.
